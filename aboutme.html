About Me

Samarth Desai is A Hindustani Classical Guitarist and Composer. He is A National Award Winner In National Youth Festival Punjab, And 5 State Award. He Started his music Journey under his father’s guidance and then went on to learn classical music from Pt.Mallikarjun Taralgatti.

There on he created his unique Hindustani classical style which led him to showcase his talent in shows like INDIA’S GOT TALENT with tHE PUNCH BAND. He has performed with legendary artists like Pt.Jayatheerth Mevundi, Hariharan, Roopkumar Rathod and many. Also performed across the whole country and abroad in Singapore, Thailand, and many.

He now leads his musical band and has composed many melodious albums.
Music has been an integral part of his life, he wants to contribute back by composing more soulful music and also by sharing his learning to many students. He wants the Hindustani Classical Fusion style reach to the nation's heart.
