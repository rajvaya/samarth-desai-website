About Guru Shishya

  • Classes are open to all.
  • Duration of each class will be 1 hour.
  • Students will have to take prior appointment by sending an email to Samarth Desai at hello@samarthdesai.in
  • The day and time of the classes will be decided by Samarth Desai and the student by mutual convenience.
  • Students are requested to send in their appointment/request emails at least 1 week before the date and time of the class required.
  • Students are expected to have their own guitars before requesting for an online appointment.
  • Classes are being conducted online through video calls. Students are expected to download the required software themselves. Hi-speed internet connection, a good headset with microphone and a silent environment, are some of the other conditions students are expected to take care of.
  • We are not responsible if ongoing classes are disrupted in between due to unforeseen circumstances. In such cases classes can be rescheduled.
  • Although these classes are designed to be one-on-one, Samarth Desai has the right to include any number of students through a group/conference call.
  • Audio/video recording, publishing of any Audio/Video/text material thereof is strictly prohibited and is deemed a violation of copyrights.
